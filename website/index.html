About Us

Who we are

African Community Northern Territory (AFCOM NT) is a newly formed not-for-profit association from Darwin and Katherine. Established in 2024 by a group of young individuals passionate about football and a shared vision of empowering African people, communities and businesses in the NT.

I

Our Vision

A community where African people and people from low socio-economic backgrounds live healthy, peacefully, connected and inspired to reach their full potential — here in Australia and around the world.

II

Our Mission

Equip individuals and groups with the confidence and courage to undertake social enterprises and economic activities that boost the Territory's economy and social life — through partnership with communities, organisations, businesses and governments.

III

Our Goal

Address health, social and economic problems faced by African people in the Northern Territory of Australia through inclusive programs, sport and community-led leadership.

Primary Values

The four principles that guide everything we do.

01

A Fair Go

Embrace mutual respect and tolerance for other cultures.

02

Diversity

Embrace the diversity of people, ideas and innovations.

03

Inspire

Encourage individuals and groups to develop self-confidence.

04

Leadership

Help young people develop team skills to reach their full potential.

AFCON NT

African Cup of Nations — Northern Territory

AFCON NT celebrates the rich heritage of African football through the Annual African Cup of Nations, hosted in Darwin. A unique platform to showcase the extraordinary football talents and diversity of African people living in the NT — and a beacon of community strength bringing together diverse audiences in shared celebration of sportsmanship and mutual respect.

Key Objectives

Beyond the scoreline.

01

Impact the health and wellbeing of young people.

02

Mentor and monitor the progress of young people in our communities.

03

Develop young leaders who can positively contribute to the NT community.

04

Partner with local businesses, organisations and government agencies to bring value to the Territory.
